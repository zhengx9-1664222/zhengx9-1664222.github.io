Interving Human

The goal of interviewing users is to learn about everything
that might infuence how the users might use what you are creating.
Good interviewing is a skill you develop with practice.
The great myth is that you need to be a good talker.
Conducting a good interview is actually about shutting up.
This can be very hard, especially when you are enthusiastic about the topic.

Remember, the people you are interviewing want to be liked.
They want to demonstrate their smarts.
When you are interviewing someone you know nothing.
You are learning a completely new and fascinating subject: that person.
